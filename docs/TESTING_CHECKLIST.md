# Milestone 1 Playtest Checklist

For every problem, export the save and record the seed shown above the Chronicle.

## Setup

- [ ] Start 1-, 2-, 3-, and 4-player games.
- [ ] Confirm each player is assigned the expected home kingdom.
- [ ] Confirm Levels 1–4 display the expected Tower defender ranges.
- [ ] Close and reopen the app; confirm autosave resumes.

## Turns and resources

- [ ] End turns with 1–15, 16–30, and 31–45 warriors; confirm food use.
- [ ] Reduce warriors and confirm excess gold is abandoned.
- [ ] Add/remove a Beast and confirm capacity changes.
- [ ] Confirm a skipped turn advances cleanly in multiplayer.
- [ ] Confirm Scout grants an extra turn.

## Locations

- [ ] Use Move repeatedly and record event frequencies over at least 100 trials.
- [ ] Test Tomb/Ruin empty, battle, and treasure outcomes.
- [ ] Test Sanctuary at each aid threshold.
- [ ] Test Citadel doubling with 4, 5, 24, and 25 warriors.
- [ ] Confirm a foreign Citadel produces an illegal-move prompt.
- [ ] Confirm Inventory uses the turn.

## Bazaar

- [ ] Buy one and several warriors.
- [ ] Buy one and several food rations.
- [ ] Buy each special item.
- [ ] Haggle successfully and until the Bazaar closes.
- [ ] Attempt an unaffordable purchase and confirm closure.

## Battles

- [ ] Continue a battle for several rounds.
- [ ] Confirm a player-round win halves brigands.
- [ ] Confirm a player-round loss removes one warrior.
- [ ] Retreat and confirm one extra warrior is lost.
- [ ] Test defeat in single-player and multiplayer.

## Keys and final battle

- [ ] Confirm no key is awarded in the home kingdom.
- [ ] Confirm one key per foreign kingdom in Brass/Silver/Gold order.
- [ ] Attempt to leave a foreign kingdom without its key.
- [ ] Use a Pegasus within a kingdom and across a frontier.
- [ ] Attempt Tower entry without all requirements.
- [ ] Fail and then solve the Riddle.
- [ ] Retreat from or lose a final battle, then return.
- [ ] Win the game.
